Our Mission

Over 25 lakh people die of hunger every year in India. Our mission is to completely remove hunger from our nation.Help us feed the need, and make miracles happen!

The Need

1/6th of the Indian population is undernourished,One in four children in India is malnourished. Your small step can help beating someone's hunger.Give what you can!

The Waste

According to the United Nations Development Programme, up to 40% of the food produced in India is wasted. About 21 million tonnes of wheat are wasted in India and 50% of all food across the world meets the same fate and never reaches the needy.
